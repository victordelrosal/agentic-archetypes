# Review Response Templates

## Positive Review (4-5 stars)

Hi [Name], thank you so much for the kind words! [Reference
specific detail from their review.] We loved having you and
look forward to seeing you again.

[Business Name]

---

## Mixed Review (3 stars)

Hi [Name], thanks for taking the time to share your feedback.
We're glad [positive aspect], and we appreciate you letting
us know about [concern]. [Specific action you're taking.]
We'd love the chance to give you a better experience next time.

[Business Name]

---

## Negative Review (1-2 stars)

Hi [Name], we're sorry to hear about your experience. [Acknowledge
the specific issue without being defensive.] We take this
seriously and would like to make it right. Please reach out to
us directly at [contact] so we can resolve this.

[Business Name]
