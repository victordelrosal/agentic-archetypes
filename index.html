The problem

You know AI can help. But between the blank prompt and a running business, there's a gap.

💭

You don't know what to ask

Claude Code is powerful, but it's a blank canvas. You open the terminal, type "hello," and then what? No structure, no workflow, no starting point.

Before: staring at a blinking cursor
After: /new-lead, /invoice, /status
🔄

Nothing carries over

Every session starts from zero. Your AI doesn't remember yesterday's clients, last week's proposals, or overdue follow-ups. You repeat yourself constantly.

Before: re-explaining context every session
After: "Morning. 2 follow-ups due, 1 proposal in draft."
🧠

AI is a tool, not a team

You use AI for one-off tasks: "write this email," "summarize that doc." But there's no coordination, no delegation, no operational layer. It's a tool, not a system.

Before: one task at a time, no big picture
After: an orchestrator that delegates to specialists

An organization in a box

Each archetype gives you a complete AI operations team: an orchestrator brain, 4 specialized agents, 5 push-button skills, and domain templates. All on a shared chassis.

your-business/
  README.md ........... human onboarding
  CLAUDE.md .......... the brain (orchestrator)
  config.yaml ........ your details
  agents/ ............. 4 AI specialists
  skills/ ............. 5 slash commands
  templates/ .......... domain docs
  .ops/ ............... state & history
  workspace/ .......... output for review

What a Monday morning looks like

Real scenarios across all five archetypes.

Freelancer

A lead goes cold. You catch it.

You open Claude Code on Monday. The system reads your state and flags: "Sarah from Acme hasn't heard from you in 12 days."

/status
"1 follow-up overdue: Sarah (Acme), 12 days."
/new-lead follow-up drafted → workspace/

Get started in 2 minutes

1

Clone the repo

git clone https://github.com/victordelrosal/agentic-archetypes.git

2

Pick your archetype

Choose the one that matches your business type: freelancer, content creator, remote worker, e-commerce, or local service.

3

Fill in config.yaml

Your name, services, rates, tools, preferences. 60 seconds. This personalizes everything.

4

Open Claude Code

cd freelancer && claude

The system detects it's your first run and walks you through onboarding. You're operational.
